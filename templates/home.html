Featurelet is....

  • An end-user oriented companion to your issue tracker
  • Driving project direction from user opinion, promoting lean development
  • A way to fund smaller open source projects and grow them rapidly

For project maintainers

Drive rapid development by having an easy feeback mechanism for end users

  • Gauge user interest with voting
  • Simple and intuitive interface lowers barrier to contribution
  • Integration with existing issue trackers makes transition smooth

Grow your open source project

  • Users can vote on improvements with a monetary contribution towards its completion in small amounts
  • Crowdfund the development of your side project or tool, keeping it alive and relevant

For end users

Help grow projects you're interested in

  • Vote on improvements relevant to your needs and track them to completion
  • Fund your favourite project with micro-contributions or regular votes

Engage in a community

  • Get notified of new releases for projects by watching them
  • Provide your feedback in a conversation with other users to established projects to help improve them

Always free for open source

Featurelet's full service is availible free of charge for open source projects.
